Hi, I'm Jana!

I was born in Serbia, I moved to Canada at age 5 and have called it home ever since. As a child, I was endlessly curious—I wanted to understand how everything worked and wouldn't stop asking questions until I got answers.

In high school, I fell in love with math and physics, which led me to pursue Engineering Physics at McMaster University, along with a minor in math. After graduating, I was ready for a change of scenery, so I moved to beautiful Montreal to pursue my Master's in Biomedical Engineering at McGill University, focusing on MRI image acquisition techniques.

And it was there that I fell in love with machine learning!

It perfectly combined all the subjects I was already passionate about: calculus, statistics, and creative problem solving. I dove deep into the field, enrolling in graduate-level courses beyond my degree requirements, including Introduction to Machine Learning, Computer Vision, Deep Learning, and Artificial Intelligence.

After graduating, I joined Siemens, where I integrate, educate, and promote generative AI applications internally. My passion and curiosity for artificial intelligence continue to drive me to learn as much as I can about this rapidly evolving field and work on exciting new projects.

But who am I beyond that?

I live a simple life. I love long walks on sunny days, exploring cities, discovering new great cafes. I'm passionate about good coffee and tea—especially matcha! I adore thoughtful urban planning and truly believe public transportation is the future. There's something magical about trains and walkable communities that just makes cities come alive. For that reason, I love exploring old, far off cities, and wonder who walked these streets and what stories they have to tell.

Community is incredibly important to me. I believe in orienting my life around giving to others in small ways. Developing relationships you can rely on and fostering genuine connections. This means being generous with your time and resources, and sometimes being inconvenienced for the good of the community. I'm also passionate about healthy eating and wellness. I cook nutritious meals every day and make my own kombucha (happy to share!). I spend time outdoors regularly and absolutely love lifting heavy weights at the gym. There's nothing quite like seeing strong, focused ladies crushing their goals!
