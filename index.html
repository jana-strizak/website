Hi, I'm Jana!

Welcome to my personal webpage! I'm so glad you're here. This is a little corner of the internet where I share my passions, projects, and thoughts with the world. Enjoy your stay!

Let’s start at the beginning…

I was born nestled in the mysterious mystical mountains of the Balkans, in a small place called Kragujevac, Serbia. Even though I moved to Canada at the young age of 5, those ancient lands of my foremothers hold a special place in my heart, and I truly believe nowhere in the world is as beautiful as my homeland. I’m so interested in the old pagan Slavic traditions of my people, and find so much joy in learning of it’s history, all the powers that rose and fell on that land, and the many many cultures that collided and influenced the culture.

As a child, I was endlessly curious about the world. I wanted to understand how everything worked and wouldn't stop asking questions. Even when I got answers, I still couldn’t stop picking at the scab for more.

look at those pretty old buildings!

In high school, I fell in love with math and physics, it somehow satisfied that curiosity itch I carried with me since childhood. This led me to pursue a degree in Engineering Physics at McMaster University, along with a minor in math. After graduating, I was ready for a change of scenery, so I moved to the marvelous Montreal to pursue my Master's in Biomedical Engineering at McGill University, focusing on MRI image acquisition techniques. Some might call my education interdisciplinary, or diverse, but to me, it’s all within my area of interest: basic physics, math, and engineering principles that govern how light travels through an optical fiber, how electricity powers our devices, or how a varying magnetic field can be used to encode position and see inside an object!

And it was there that I fell in love with machine learning too!

Okay I’ll be honest; I didn’t know much about it until my masters. But it perfectly combined all the subjects I was already passionate about: linear algebra, statistics, and creative problem solving. I dove deep into the field, enrolling in graduate-level courses beyond my degree requirements, including Introduction to Machine Learning, Computer Vision, Deep Learning, and Artificial Intelligence.

After graduating, I joined Siemens, where I integrate, educate, and promote generative AI applications internally. My passion and curiosity for artificial intelligence continue to drive me to learn as much as I can about this rapidly evolving field and work on exciting new projects.

But who am I beyond that?

I live a simple life. I love long walks on sunny days, exploring cities, discovering new great cafes, I love a good scent, I love a dramatic soiree at improv, or a competitive little game of chess. I'm passionate about good coffee and tea—especially matcha! I adore thoughtful urban planning and truly believe public transportation is the future. There's something magical about trains and walkable communities that just makes cities come alive. For that reason, I love exploring old, far off cities, and wonder who walked these streets and what stories they have to tell.

Community is incredibly important to me. I believe in orienting my life around giving to others in small ways. Developing relationships you can rely on and fostering genuine connections. This means being generous with your time and resources, and sometimes being inconvenienced for the good of the community. I'm also passionate about healthy eating and wellness. I cook nutritious meals every day and make my own kombucha (happy to share!). I spend time outdoors regularly and absolutely love lifting heavy weights at the gym. There's nothing quite like seeing strong, focused ladies crushing their goals!
